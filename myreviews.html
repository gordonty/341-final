My Reviews

Below are some of my thoughts/ reviews on a few books that I have read. The reviews are not that good so no judging.

Post-apocalyptic

Matched Trilogy by Ally Condie

An Interseting love story of a young woman who defies the rules of government to find her own love. I felt that they were very similar to other post-apocalyptic books but they are still a fairly good read.

The Selection Series by Kiera Cass

Wonderful love story of a girl who has to compete for the princes’ affection and together they change their city. Very good and captivating read. There are several novellas as well so you will have plenty to read.

The Hunger Games by Suzanne Collins

A young woman defies the government to bring hope to people while fighting for her life. Good reads but more for the older kids as it does involve people killing each other for entertainment. It makes you think about the human race a little.

Fantasy

Twilight Series by Stephanie Meyer

Young woman falls in love with vampire while her best friend is a werewolf. It is a love story but there is plenty of fighting for those who aren’t complete fans of the romance such as myself. Pretty good read but not much character development.

Harry Potter Series by J.K. Rowling

Wonderful read full of magic, friendship, and adventure. These books are full of adventure that can teach people about friendship, family, hope, and much more. If you are looking to escape for a while these are the books for you.

Merlin Series by T.A. Barron

I will admit I have not read the whole series yet. These books are an adventure of a young merliln before his service to King Arthur story. It's an interesting take on merlin's side of the King Arthur story.

Tiger's Curse Series by Colleen Houck

Young women helps two Indian princes break their curse while falling in love and figuring out who to choose. This series has some Indian myths and beliefs which make it all the more interesting and gives you a taste of another culture. I couldn't put these books down.

Crime

The Poisoner's Handbook by Deborah Blum

This book talks about different poisons and how forensic medicine came to be. It's interesting to read if you like learning about true crime and how forensic science developed. You do have to understand a little bit of chemistry to understand parts of this book.

Fatal Voyage by Kathy Reichs

If you like the TV show Bones you will enjoy this book as it has a connection to the show. It's a good read. You can try solve the crime along with the characters.
