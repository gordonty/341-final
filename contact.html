About The Website

This website is/was my final project for an intro to web design class. This website is for individuals who like to read or are interested in reading. I am hoping this website will help bring individuals who like to read together in some way and help them find something to read.

About Me

I love to read, it's one of my favorite things to do. Sometimes however I struggle to find something to read or I'm not sure the book I decided to read is any good so I created this website in hopes that it will help others who get into those same situations.
