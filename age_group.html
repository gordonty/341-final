Early Younger Readers

Here are some suggestions for younger readers such as those in early elementary school.

  • Junie B. Jones Collection by Barbara Park
  • Animal Ark Series by Ben M. Baglio
  • Matilda by Roald Dahl
  • Choose Your Own Adventure by R.A. Montgomery, Shannon Gilligan, and Jay Leibold
    • The different books are witten by the different authors but the majority of them are written by R.A Montgomery
  • Stealing Thunder by Mary Casanova
Late Younger Readers

Here are some suggestions for younger readers such as those later in elementary school or early middle shcool.

  • The Chronicles of Narnia by C.S. Lewis
  • A Series of Unfortunate Events by Lemony Snicket
  • The tail of Emily Windsnap by Liz Kessler
  • Princess Academy by Shannon Hale
  • Stranded by Ben Mikaelsen
  • Peter Pan by J.M Barrie
  • The Theif Lord by Cornelia Funke
Young Adult Readers

Here are some suggestions for young adult readers such as those later in middle school or early in high school

  • The Selection Series by Kiera Cass
  • The Tiger's Curse Series by Colleen Houck
  • The Mortal Instruments Series by Casandra Clare
  • The Guardian Chronicles by Breanne R. Winkle
    • There is only one book out so far but hopefully the author will be getting other completed soon
  • Miss Peregrine's Home for Pecular Children by Ransom Riggs
  • The Matched Series by Ally Condie
  • The Hourglass Door Trilogy by Lisa Mangum
Older Readers

Here are some suggestions for older readers such as late high school and adults

  • Lord of the Rings by J.R.R. Tolkien
  • The Davinci Code by Dan Brown
  • The Fatal Voyage by Kathy Reichs
  • The Poisoner's Handbook by Deborah Blum
As always many of these book can be enjoyed by people of any age.
